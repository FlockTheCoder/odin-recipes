Blueberry Cake - you won't find a better one!

Description

This really awesome cake is vegan and glutenfree, and there is no sugar added. Its flour is made of bananas, oats & applesauce with a bit of of cashew butter.

The recipe is based on the Blueberry Banana Bread by Anthony William.

I stripped it off any added sugar, added other fruit sources and adjusted the other ingredients until it was perfect. That is how I like it best - give it a try!

Ingredients

  • 400g ripe banans
  • 2 teaspoons backing soda
  • 3 teaspoons gingerbread spice
  • 2 tablespoons cashew butter
  • 3 tablespoons apple sauce
  • 350g oat flour
  • 100 ml oat milk or water
  • 300g wild blueberries
